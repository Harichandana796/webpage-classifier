<h3 class="wdn-brand">Applying for Financial Aid</h3>
<h4 class="wdn-brand">Prospective Students</h4>
<p>Applying for all university, college and departmental scholarships is part of the application for admission. No additional steps are needed, you will be
            considered for any scholarships you qualify for – however, don't miss the application deadline. In addition students also have various other financial aid options available, visit the financial aid office for more information.</p>
<p><a class="wdn-button wdn-button-brand" href="http://financialaid.unl.edu">Office of financial aid</a></p>
<h4 class="wdn-brand">Current Undergraduate Students</h4>
<p>Submit the completed application online by February 1. Email <a href="mailto:jenna.huttenmaier@unl.edu">Jenna Huttenmaier</a> if you have any questions on
            scholarships and supporting materials required for certain scholarships.</p>
<div class="wdn-grid-set zenbox content-columns component equal-column-height">
<div class="bp2-wdn-col-one-third border-right">
<p><span class="number" style="line-height: 0.6em">1</span>  Log into your MyRED account and click 'Scholarship application for current undergraduate
                    students.'
                </p>
</div>
<div class="bp2-wdn-col-one-third border-right">
<p><span class="number" style="line-height: 0.6em">2</span>  Include all required and optional material. Contact <a href="mailto:jenna.huttenmaier@unl.edu">Jenna</a>                    if you have questions on supporting material.</p>
</div>
<div class="bp2-wdn-col-one-third">
<p><span class="number" style="line-height: 0.6em">3</span>  Complete the Free Application for Federal Student Aid (FAFSA) at www.fafsa.gov.</p>
</div>
</div>
<h4 class="wdn-brand">Current Graduate Students</h4>
<p>Several graduate fellowships are awarded by the Department of Computer Science &amp; Engineering. Application procedures can be found by visiting the Office of Graduate Studies.</p>
<p><a class="wdn-button wdn-button-brand" href="http://www.unl.edu/gradstudies/current/funding">Office of Graduate Studies</a>
</p></div>

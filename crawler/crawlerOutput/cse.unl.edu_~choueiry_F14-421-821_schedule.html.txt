<b><font color="#0045be">Topic:</font> </b> Basics of Constraint-based Scheduling, <i>Nicolas Bonifas</i><br/>
We will introduce a framework in which to model a generic class of
scheduling problems using constraint programming, and explain why
scheduling is one of the most successful application of constraint
programming. We will also give a brief introduction to the modeling
paradigm used in the IBM Ilog CP Optimizer solver.<br/><br/>
<b><font color="#0045be">Required reading:</font></b> Philippe Laborie
and Jerome Rogerie. <a href="Documents/Laborie-FLAIRS08.pdf" target="_blank">Reasoning with Conditional Time-Intervals</a>. FLAIRS
conference, 555-560. 2008.<br/><br/>
<b><font color="#0045be">Recommended reading:</font></b>
<ul>
<li>Philippe Baptiste, Claude Le Pape, and Wim
Nuijten. Constraint-Based Scheduling: Applying Constraint Programming
to Scheduling Problems. International Series in Operations Research
and Management Science. Kluwer, 2001. <i>(Available from Engineering Library)</i></li>
<li>Philippe Laborie. <a href="Documents/Laborie-CPAIOR-2009.pdf" target="_blank">IBM ILOG CP Optimizer for detailed scheduling
illustrated on three problems</a>. Integration of AI and OR Techniques
in Constraint Programming for Combinatorial Optimization Problems,
148-162. Springer Berlin Heidelberg, 2008.</li>
<li>Abder Aggoun and Nicolas Beldiceanu. <a href="Documents/Aggoun-Beldiceanu-1993.pdf" target="_blank">Extending
CHIP in order to solve complex scheduling and placement
problems</a>. Math. Comput. Model., 17(7):57-73, April 1993.</li>
</ul>

<br/>
<b><font color="#0045be">Topic:</font> </b> Propagating the Cumulative
Constraint, <i>Nicolas Bonifas</i><br/> 

The cumulative constraint is at the heart of constraint-based
scheduling and one of the most famous global constraint. We will give
an overview of the different propagation techniques for the cumulative
constraint, exposing the trade-offs between propagation speed and
propagation strength.
<br/>
<br/>
<b><font color="#0045be">Required reading:</font></b> Abder Aggoun and
Nicolas Beldiceanu. <a href="Documents/AggounBeldiceanu93.pdf" target="_blank">Extending chip in order to
solve complex scheduling and placement
problems</a>. Math. Comput. Model., 17(7):57-73, April 1993.
<br/>
<br/>
<b><font color="#0045be">Recommended reading:</font></b>
<ul>
<li>Erschler, J., and Lopez, P. 1990. <a href="Documents/Erschler-Lopez-1991.pdf" target="_blank"> Energy-based approach for task scheduling under time
and resources constraints</a>. In 2nd international workshop on
project management and scheduling, 115-121.</li>
<li>
Schutt, A., Feydy, T., Stuckey, P., and Wallace, M. <a href="Documents/SchuttConstraints2011.pdf" target="_blank">Explaining

2011.
</li>

</li>

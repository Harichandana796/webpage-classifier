<p class="MsoNormal">Dear Invitee:</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><o:p> </o:p></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-indent:.5in">We would like to invite you to a
workshop on <span class="SpellE"><b><span style="font-size:11.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:
10.0pt">Phenology</span></b></span><b><span style="font-size:11.0pt;mso-bidi-font-size:
10.0pt"> and Environmental Change:<span style="mso-spacerun:yes"> 
</span>Challenges and Opportunities.</span></b><span style="mso-spacerun:yes">   </span>The workshop abstract and program are given
below.<span style="mso-spacerun:yes">  </span>Additional information on the
workshop can be found at <span style="mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;color:black"><a href="http://csce.unl.edu/phenology">http://csce.unl.edu/phenology</a></span><span style="font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Arial;color:black"> </span><span style="mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt;color:black">in the events tab.</span><span style="font-size:10.0pt;font-family:Arial;color:black"><span style="mso-spacerun:yes">  </span></span>The workshop will begin on Thursday,
June 16 at <st1:time hour="8" minute="30">8:30 A.M.</st1:time> (registration at
<st1:time hour="19" minute="30">7:30</st1:time>) and end at <st1:time hour="12" minute="0">noon</st1:time> on <st1:date day="17" month="6" year="2005">Friday,
 June 17, 2005</st1:date>.<span style="mso-spacerun:yes">  </span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-indent:.5in"><o:p> </o:p></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-indent:.5in">The intended audience for the workshop
is scientists who are interested in <span class="SpellE">phenology</span>,
climate change, spatial data mining, and trying to tie all of them together to
create an understanding of how ecosystems change with time.<span style="mso-spacerun:yes">  </span>The workshop should provide an excellent
learning opportunity, as well as a venue for the exchange of ideas among a
highly interdisciplinary group of scientists.<span style="mso-spacerun:yes"> 
</span>You may register at <span style='font-size:10.0pt;font-family:"Courier New"'><a href="https://busfin-s.unl.edu/conferences/pec/reg.cfm">https://busfin-s.unl.edu/conferences/pec/reg.cfm</a>
</span>.<span style="mso-spacerun:yes">  </span>The registration fees are
$35.00 for members of the University of Nebraska and $75.00 for attendees from
other institutions.<span style="mso-spacerun:yes">  </span>The registration fee
includes refreshments at breaks, a luncheon, and program materials.<span style="mso-spacerun:yes">  </span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-indent:.5in"><o:p> </o:p></p>
<p class="MsoNormal" style="text-indent:.5in">The workshop will be held at the
Cornhusker Hotel (<span style="mso-bidi-font-size:10.0pt"><a href="http://www.thecornhusker.com/index.sp">http://www.thecornhusker.com/index.sp</a>)
</span>in <st1:place><st1:city>Lincoln</st1:city>, <st1:state>NE.</st1:state></st1:place><span style="mso-spacerun:yes">  </span>Please make your room reservation (<span lang="EN" style="mso-ansi-language:EN">800-793-7474)</span> with the hotel so you
will receive the discounted workshop room rate of $89 (1 to 4
persons/night).<span style="mso-spacerun:yes">  </span>Please tell the hotel
you are attending the <span class="SpellE">Phenology</span> University of
Nebraska Workshop.<span style="mso-spacerun:yes">  </span>Please make your
reservations early because there are a limited number of rooms available at
this rate.<span style="mso-spacerun:yes">   </span>The Cornhusker Hotel is in
downtown <st1:city><st1:place>Lincoln</st1:place></st1:city> and has an
excellent shuttle service to and from the airport, as well as easy access to
the amenities of <st1:city><st1:place>Lincoln</st1:place></st1:city>.</p>

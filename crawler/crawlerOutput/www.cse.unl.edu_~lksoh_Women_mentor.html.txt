<hr/><br/>

The best mentors should be good listeners, observers, and problems solvers,
approachable and available, supportive and understanding.

<br/><br/>

There are various features that people could combine into an effective
program:

<ul><li>Formal vs. Informal or Spontaneous</li></ul>
<ul><li>Long vs. Short Term</li></ul>
<ul><li>On Site vs. Long Distance</li></ul>
<ul><li>Interactive vs. Observational</li></ul>
<ul><li>Present Day vs. Historical</li></ul>
<ul><li>Instructor Mentor vs. Peer Mentor vs. Work-force Mentor</li></ul>
<ul><li>One vs. Multiple Mentors or a Team</li></ul>
<br/><br/>

Topics to discuss:
<ul><li>Topics pertaining to the personal lives of mentees</li></ul>
<ul><li>Topics involving general school-related matters</li></ul>
<ul><li>Topics concerning graduate school</li></ul>
<ul><li>Topics oriented toward career matters</li></ul>

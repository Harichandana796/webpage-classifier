<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-bottom:6.0pt"><span class="SpellE"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight:normal"><span style='font-size:16.0pt;font-family:
"Eras Medium ITC";color:#99CC00'>GroupChat</span></b></span><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight:normal"><span style='font-size:16.0pt;font-family:
"Eras Medium ITC";color:#99CC00'><o:p></o:p></span></b></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style='font-family:"Eras Medium ITC"'>This <span class="SpellE">GroupChat</span> capability was completed in August 2005, and was
later released together with the Conference XP Platform.<span style="mso-spacerun:yes">  </span>This capability basically allows a user to
view incoming messages and to send messages.<span style="mso-spacerun:yes"> 
</span>In addition, this capability allows a user to manage different group
discussions and target specific messages for different groups.<span style="mso-spacerun:yes">  </span>The capability automatically filters the
messages exchanged in the CXP venue for users in the proper group.<span style="mso-spacerun:yes">  </span><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><o:p> </o:p></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style='font-family:"Eras Medium ITC"'>Specific
features of this capability include:<o:p></o:p></span></p>
<ul style="margin-top:0in" type="disc">
<li class="MsoNormal" style="mso-list:l0 level1 lfo1;tab-stops:list .5in"><span style='font-family:"Eras Medium ITC"'>Text-based forum<o:p></o:p></span></li>
<li class="MsoNormal" style="mso-list:l0 level1 lfo1;tab-stops:list .5in"><span style='font-family:"Eras Medium ITC"'>View of messages<o:p></o:p></span></li>
<li class="MsoNormal" style="mso-list:l0 level1 lfo1;tab-stops:list .5in"><span style='font-family:"Eras Medium ITC"'>Forum group management (join a
     group, leave a group, register a new group, remove an existing group)<o:p></o:p></span></li>
<li class="MsoNormal" style="mso-list:l0 level1 lfo1;tab-stops:list .5in"><span style='font-family:"Eras Medium ITC"'>Group-based message broadcast<o:p></o:p></span></li>
<li class="MsoNormal" style="mso-list:l0 level1 lfo1;tab-stops:list .5in"><span style='font-family:"Eras Medium ITC"'>Individual user-specific messaging<o:p></o:p></span></li>
<li class="MsoNormal" style="mso-list:l0 level1 lfo1;tab-stops:list .5in"><span style='font-family:"Eras Medium ITC"'>Review of group messages<o:p></o:p></span></li>
<li class="MsoNormal" style="mso-list:l0 level1 lfo1;tab-stops:list .5in"><span style='font-family:"Eras Medium ITC"'>Multiple concurrent group forums<o:p></o:p></span></li>
</ul>

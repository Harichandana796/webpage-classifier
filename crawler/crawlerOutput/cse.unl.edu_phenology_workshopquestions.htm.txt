<h2 align="center" style="text-align:center"><span style="font-size:14.0pt;
mso-bidi-font-size:12.0pt">Possible Research Questions<o:p></o:p></span></h2>
<p class="MsoNormal"><o:p> </o:p></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">Can we use phenological data to address (quantify, predict)
issues of climate change?<i><span style="mso-spacerun:yes">  </span></i>Can a
relationship between phenology and climate change be substantiated?<span style="mso-spacerun:yes">  </span>How many years of data and what is the
quality of data needed to answer the above questions?<span style="mso-spacerun:yes">  </span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><o:p> </o:p></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">What phenological measurements should be taken that would
greatly improve our data sets and potential data analysis?<span style="mso-spacerun:yes">  </span>Can spring sown crop data be used or must we
use information from only fall sown crops and perennials?</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><o:p> </o:p></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">Can we develop (validate) better management strategies for
the managed ecosystems using phenology? Are there signals between the unmanaged
ecosystems (e.g. migratory bird patterns) and the managed ecosystems (planting
date, etc.) that can be used to enhance ecosystem management?<span style="mso-spacerun:yes">  </span>What would be the economic impact or
environmental impact of having this improved knowledge?</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><o:p> </o:p></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">How can advances in computer science, data mining, and
spatial data be used to tie together our phenological data to climate change
and climate variation? </p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><o:p> </o:p></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">What can be learned about the relationship between
temperature and photo period effects? (Different cultivars differ in the role
of temperature vs. photo period.)?</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><o:p> </o:p></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">How should we educate others and ourselves?<span style="mso-spacerun:yes">  </span>Are there web-based tools/games that would be
useful for better public understanding of the issues of phenology and climate
variation?<span style="mso-spacerun:yes">  </span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><o:p> </o:p></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">How can the outcomes be framed to avoid ideological
differences and focus on scientific discussions?</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><o:p> </o:p></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">How can our group augment, supplement, collaborate with the
ongoing national efforts in phenology?</p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><i><o:p> </o:p></i></p>
<p class="MsoNormal">If we wanted to create a signature program in phenology at
the University of Nebraska, what would it be and how would we approach funding?</p>

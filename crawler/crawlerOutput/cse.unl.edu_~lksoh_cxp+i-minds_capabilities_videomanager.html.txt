<p class="MsoNormal" style="margin-bottom:6.0pt"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight:
normal"><span style='font-size:16.0pt;font-family:"Eras Medium ITC";color:#99CC00'>VideoManager
and VideoManagerPlus<o:p></o:p></span></b></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style='font-family:"Eras Medium ITC"'>This VideoManager
capability was completed in October 2005.<span style="mso-spacerun:yes"> 
</span>This capability basically allows a user to manage video streams,
allowing the user to dictate whose videos would be broadcast to other venue
users, and allowing other users to request for their videos to be
broadcast.<span style="mso-spacerun:yes">  </span>This capability allows an
instructor to choose which students’ videos to be shared and the number of
videos broadcast at any given time.<span style="mso-spacerun:yes">  </span><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style='font-family:"Eras Medium ITC"'><o:p> </o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style='font-family:"Eras Medium ITC"'>The
VideoManagerPlus capability was later completed in November 2005.<span style="mso-spacerun:yes">  </span>Similar to its predecessor, the only
improvement is that it allows for actual blocking of video streams, cutting
down the video traffic in the venue effectively.<span style="mso-spacerun:yes">   </span><o:p></o:p></span></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><o:p> </o:p></p>
<p class="MsoNormal"><span style='font-family:"Eras Medium ITC"'>Specific
features of this capability include:<o:p></o:p></span></p>
<ul style="margin-top:0in" type="disc">
<li class="MsoNormal" style="mso-list:l0 level1 lfo1;tab-stops:list .5in"><span style='font-family:"Eras Medium ITC"'>Tiled video streams (based on CXP)<o:p></o:p></span></li>
<li class="MsoNormal" style="mso-list:l0 level1 lfo1;tab-stops:list .5in"><span style='font-family:"Eras Medium ITC"'>Manager’s option on whose video
     stream to be blocked/unblocked<o:p></o:p></span></li>
<li class="MsoNormal" style="mso-list:l0 level1 lfo1;tab-stops:list .5in"><span style='font-family:"Eras Medium ITC"'>User’s option to request for his/her
     video stream to be unblocked<o:p></o:p></span></li>
<li class="MsoNormal" style="mso-list:l0 level1 lfo1;tab-stops:list .5in"><span style='font-family:"Eras Medium ITC"'>VideoManager:<span style="mso-spacerun:yes">  </span>video streams “blocked” are still
     archived digitally by CXP, allowing users to go back and review all video
     streams afterwards.<o:p></o:p></span></li>
<li class="MsoNormal" style="mso-list:l0 level1 lfo1;tab-stops:list .5in"><span style='font-family:"Eras Medium ITC"'>VideoManagerPlus:<span style="mso-spacerun:yes">  </span>video streams “blocked” are not archived
     digitally by CXP, allowing the system to prevent all irrelevant or
     undesirable video streams from being archived.<o:p></o:p></span></li>
</ul>

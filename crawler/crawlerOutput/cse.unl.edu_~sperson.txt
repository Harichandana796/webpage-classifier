<h3 style="margin: 0.0px 0.0px 14.0px 0.0px; font: 18.0px 'Times New Roman'"><b>About Me</b></h3>
<p class="p7">I am an Associate Professor of Practice in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the University of Nebraska-Lincoln. I am also the Director of the new Software Engineering program that will launch in Fall 2016 (contact me if you would like to learn more about the new undergraduate degree in Software Engineering at UNL - we are very excited about offering this major!). I worked in industry and state government as a Software Engineer for many years. Prior to coming to UNL I was a Research Computer Scientist at NASA Langley Research Center where I worked in the Formal Methods group. You can view a copy of my <a href="PersonCV.pdf">CV here</a>.</p>
<p class="p7">My research interests include software testing and program analysis techniques, especially in the context of evolving software.</p>

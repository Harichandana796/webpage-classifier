<p>
<b>Current Research Interests</b>
</p><ul>
<li>Informatics and chemometrics for multi-dimensional chemical separations
and three-dimensional mass-spectrometric imaging.</li>
<li>Image restoration, resampling, and data fusion for infrared images
and remotely sensed, multispectral data.
</li><li>Digital image processing algorithms and architectures that integrate
restoration, compression, coding, and enhancement for optimal end-to-end
performance subject to explicit implementation constraints.
</li><li>Scene modeling and artificial scene generation.
Imaging system design, analysis, and simulation.
Research methodology for digital image processing and computer vision.
</li><li>Visual information processing in multimedia systems, especially for
web-based decision support.
</li></ul>
